Pacio Vision

The Pacio Vision is to transform accounting and real time management.

Cryptocurrency, decentralisation, blockchain, distributed ledger, and smart contract technologies are bringing revolutionary change to business, government, and society, leading to a safer, more inclusive, and more productive future. Pacio will enable the revolution to deliver its full benefit to the world.

Pacio will do this via a blockchain based accounting and real time management engine, which provides better, content based (semantic) ways to store and process accounting and real time management information for use by any application. This will include an authenticated data feed for oracle and app use.

Pacio will include support for traditional account methods, with bridges to the new.

Paco will bypass the much talked about but little seen semantic web to leap forward to the accountable semantic blockchain instead, with tools to make it all easy for use in daily life.

An API (Application Programming Interface) will allow access to and use of Pacio’s power by any app, wherever it runs. App developers will be able to focus on meeting their market requirements, without needing to reinvent all the accounting and real time management systems available via Pacio. This will allow better, more consistent (standardised), more useful applications to be developed faster.

The Pacio contribution to the state of the art will be developed from the life time body of work of the founders, as the greatest advance in accounting and real time management since the invention of double entry bookkeeping 500 years ago.

Pacio crypto transactions and some basic API ops will be free, but more sophisticated API use will involve a fee, to be paid for in the Pacio cryptocurrency, the PIO. In accordance with the Pacio philosophy of openness and transparency, PIOs will be numbered like bank notes and be fully traceable, to provide a clear trail in the event of theft. The PIO will be a safe cryptocurrency with a real purpose, which should prove attractive as a general use cryptocurrency beyond direct Pacio use.

In conjunction with the blockchain revolution in progress Pacio will help increase safety online, expand opportunities, return ownership of data to its creators, improve productivity, give better bottom-line business outcomes, reduce fraud, and help with better governance everywhere.
